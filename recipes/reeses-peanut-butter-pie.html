Reese's Peanut Butter Pie




Description

This rich and delicious Reese's Peanut Butter Pie recipe is inspired by the famous candy and easy to make, requiring no oven or baking.
Using only a few simple ingredients and a stand or hand mixer, you can make this delectable treat for any gathering.

It is important to use high quality ingredients, since they will be the star of the show and determine how well your pie comes out in the end.
Whipping your cream using a cold metal bowl and a cold whisk will ensure good stiff peaks.


Ingredients

  • 1 Oreo or Chocolate pie crust
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 1 3/4 cup powdered sugar, divided as below
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 8 oz. cream cheese, softened
  • 1 3/4 cups peanut butter
  • 20 miniature Reeses cups, coarsly chopped, for topping (optional)
  • Small amount melted peanut butter, for topping (optional)
  • Small amount hot fudge, for topping (optional)

Steps

  1. Add heavy whipping cream, 3/4 cup powdered sugar and vanilla extract to cold bowl and whip until stiff peaks form.
  2. Cream together softened cream cheese, remaining 1 cup of powdered sugar and all of the peanut butter.
  3. Fold half of the whipped cream into the peanut butter mixture.
  4. Pour mixture into pit crust.
  5. Top with remaining whipped cream. Add optional toppings if desired.
  6. Chill in fridge for at least 2 hours.
